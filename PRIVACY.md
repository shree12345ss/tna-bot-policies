Privacy Policy

The TNA Discord Bot respects user privacy.

1. The bot may collect and store:
   - Discord User IDs
   - Server IDs
   - Moderation actions (mute/timeout logs)
2. No personal information (emails, passwords, messages) is collected.
3. Data is used strictly to provide bot functionality.
4. Data is never sold or shared with third parties.
5. Users may request data removal by contacting the bot owner.

By using the bot, you consent to this privacy policy.
